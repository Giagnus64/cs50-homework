0. Besides 4 × 4 (which are Game of Fifteen’s dimensions), what other dimensions does the framework allow?
The game allows frameworks from 3x3 up to 9x9.

With what sort of data structure is the game’s board represented?
The game's board is presented in an array of a square of the user's input. i.e. Userinput = 3; 3X3 array.

What function is called to greet the player at game’s start?
greet();

What functions do you apparently need to implement?
init(); draw(); move(); won();
